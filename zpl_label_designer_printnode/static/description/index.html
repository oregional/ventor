Direct Print PRO requires External Print Service

One of the products in this bundle includes paid subscription for external printing service

In order to allow direct printing independent on where your Odoo is located (hosting, odoo.sh, your local server) and where your printers are located, it is mandatory to have external print service. So our module can communicate with it and send print commands to it. See the video explanation of how it works
